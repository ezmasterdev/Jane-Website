About Me

A mom of two wonderful kids. I worked in an office for over seven years, where I learned a lot. But in 2020, when the pandemic started, I began a new adventure as a part-time virtual assistant, working from home. It was new to me, but I really enjoyed it. By 2022, I decided to leave my office job and work as a freelancer full-time. It was a bit scary to change, but also very exciting. I learned many new things and stayed positive through the ups and downs. Just like a roller coaster ride, I kept going and enjoyed the journey. Now, I use everything I learned in my previous job and as a mom in my work as a virtual assistant. I love learning and growing in this job, and I'm excited about what's coming next!

Why choose me?

In my job, I've worked on lots of different projects, more than a hundred of them. Each one was like a small adventure. I used everything I know to make each project the best it can be, like putting together a puzzle. The best part of my job is not just finishing these projects, but seeing how happy my clients are when we're done. It's like making friends and helping them at the same time. Each project is a new story, and I love being part of it. It's fun and rewarding, and I can't wait to see what comes next.
